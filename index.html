Who are we?

Token of Mercy is a Community Based Organization founded in 2020 and registered under certificate number. Our main aim is to eradicate gender disparities in education, health and human rights. Our interventions are rested in Western Kenya in Kisumu county. Currently, we work in Nyalenda, Kisumu west and South west Seme. Our aim is to engage the community as a whole through linkages with schools and community dialogues aimed at creating awareness on human rights, climate change, sexual and gender based violence and peaceful co-existence. As an organization, we are non-partisan while addressing issues of human rights and gender but we embrace inclusivity.

PROJECT AREAS

Education

Education is the bedrock of our interventions. We work closely with schools in Kisumu County to ensure that children, especially girls, ... have access to quality education.Our initiatives include scholarship programs, school supplies donations, and after-school tutoring. By addressing barriers such as poverty, early marriages, and cultural biases, we strive to keep girls in school and support their academic success. Education is not only a fundamental human right but also a critical lever for sustainable development..
Women and Youth empowerment
Empowering women and youth is a cornerstone of our mission at Token of Mercy. We believe that by providing opportunities for education... skill development, and leadership, we can foster a generation capable of driving positive change within their communities. Our programs focus on equipping women and young people with the tools they need to become self-reliant and influential members of society. Through workshops, mentorship, and training sessions, we help them build the confidence and skills necessary to participate actively in economic, social, and political arenas.
Therapeutic art and counselling
In addressing the psychosocial needs of our community, Token of Mercy has integrated therapeutic art and counseling into our programs... We recognize the impact of trauma and stress, particularly among women and youth who have faced gender-based violence and other adversities. Through art therapy, we provide a creative outlet for individuals to express their emotions and experiences, promoting healing and mental well-being. Additionally, our counseling services offer professional support to those in need, helping them navigate their challenges and build resilience.
Health
Health is a vital component of our work. We advocate for improved healthcare access and services, focusing on maternal health, reproductive health,... and the prevention of communicable diseases. Our health campaigns educate the community about nutrition, hygiene, and disease prevention, aiming to reduce health disparities and improve overall well-being. By partnering with local health facilities and mobilizing community health workers, we ensure that vital health information and services reach even the most remote areas.
Gender and Human rights
At the heart of our mission is the commitment to gender equality and human rights. We conduct awareness campaigns and training sessions to educate... the community about their rights and the importance of gender equality. Our efforts aim to dismantle harmful practices and stereotypes that perpetuate discrimination and violence against women and marginalized groups. By empowering individuals with knowledge and advocating for policy changes, we strive to create an inclusive society where everyone’s rights are respected and protected.
Peace and Security
Promoting peace and security is essential for creating a stable and prosperous community. Token of Mercy facilitates dialogues and conflict resolution workshops... to address underlying tensions and foster peaceful coexistence. Our peacebuilding initiatives engage various community stakeholders, including local leaders, youth groups, and women’s organizations, to collaboratively develop strategies for conflict prevention and resolution. By nurturing a culture of peace, we contribute to the safety and stability of Kisumu County.
