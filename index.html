Who are we?

Token of Mercy is a Community Based Organization founded in 2020 and registered under certificate number. Our main aim is to eradicate gender disparities in education, health and human rights. Our interventions are rested in Western Kenya in Kisumu county. Currently, we work in Nyalenda, Kisumu west and South west Seme. Our aim is to engage the community as a whole through linkages with schools and community dialogues aimed at creating awareness on human rights, climate change, sexual and gender based violence and peaceful co-existence. As an organization, we are non-partisan while addressing issues of human rights and gender but we embrace inclusivity.
