Token of Mercy is a Community Based Organization founded in 2020 and registered. Our main aim is to eradicate gender disparities in education, health and human rights. Our interventions are rested in Western Kenya in Kisumu county. Currently, we work in Nyalenda, Kisumu west and South west Seme. Our aim is to engage the community as a whole through linkages with schools and community dialogues aimed at creating awareness on human rights, climate change, sexual and gender based violence and peaceful co-existence. As an organization, we are non-partisan while addressing issues of human rights and gender but we embrace inclusivity.

Mission

Working with OVCs, and their families in response to their to their well-being and socio-economic development.

Vision

To empower orphans and vulnerable children, men and women in educational, socio-economic and development activities..

Goals

Raise awareness on health issues, including reproductive health and mental health, through community outreach and education.
