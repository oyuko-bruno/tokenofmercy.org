  • CAUSES

    • Pupils in primary schools receive tuition support and necessary educational support materials like books, school bags and school shoes. Youths receive tuition support to enable them to access high school education. After high school, the support continues to TVET institutions, colleges and universities. .

    Both boys and girls participate in various empowerment programs. Both programs offer vital guidance and the necessary life skills that assist them to navigate life easily. Women and adolescents mothers also participate in empowerment programs aimed at making them financially independent.

    The community is engaged through after school programs and community dialogues to create awareness on gender and human rights. After school programs target adolescent girls through the safe sex program that seeks inform girls on ways to make safe choices on their sex life.

    Through after school programs and community engagements, we hold peace talks that promote cohesion and peaceful existence in our communities.

    A good umber of girls miss school when they are in their menstrual period. We provide sanitary towels and panties for girls to enable them have uninterrupted school term. This has in turn increased their performance in school as they do not miss their lessons within the term. The boys also receive inner wears and vests .

    The safe spaces available in various places allow the members of the community to benefit from therapeutic art services aimed at supporting their mental health. These are done though different art forms like music, visual arts , story telling and poetry. Counselling services also available.
